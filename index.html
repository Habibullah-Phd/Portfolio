Research Interests
🔬

Membrane Technology

Design of high-performance polymer membranes via NIPS and phase-inversion methods for selective transport and separation.

🧬

Biodegradable Polymers

Developing eco-friendly biodegradable polymer systems for sustainable membrane and biomedical applications.

⚗️

Separation

Ultrafiltration, nanofiltration, and reverse osmosis with enhanced flux, rejection, and anti-fouling properties.

♻️

Wastewater

Membrane and hybrid treatment systems for industrial effluents, with focus on textile and dye-laden wastewater.

🌱

Nanomaterials
